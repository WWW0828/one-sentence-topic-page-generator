2026 FIFA World Cup Kicks Off at Estadio Azteca

The 2026 FIFA World Cup — the first to feature 48 teams and the first co-hosted by three nations (Canada, Mexico and the USA) — opened on June 11, 2026, with host Mexico facing South Africa at the renovated Estadio Azteca in Mexico City. The expanded tournament spans 16 host cities and 104 matches, and the opening match marked the third time Estadio Azteca has staged a World Cup opener.
