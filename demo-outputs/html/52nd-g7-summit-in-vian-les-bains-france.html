Political France · 2026 G7 Presidency

52nd G7 Summit
Évian-les-Bains

The 52nd G7 Summit takes place from June 15 to 17, 2026, in the French lakeside resort of Évian-les-Bains, hosted by French President Emmanuel Macron under France's 2026 G7 Presidency. The agenda spans geopolitical challenges, peace and security for Ukraine and Europe, the situation in the Middle East, trade, and climate action. With the venue near the Swiss border, French and Swiss authorities have imposed tight security and border restrictions ahead of expected protests.
